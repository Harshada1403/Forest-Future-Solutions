I am the forest, ancient and wise, providing shelter, sustenance and oxygen For eons, I've witnessed civilizations rise and fall nurturing life in its purest form. But now, I am struggling.

Climate change, pollution, and deforestation are ravaging me Temperatures soar, seasons shift unpredictably, droughts parch my soil and floods wash away habitats Pollution poisons my waters and air, and deforestation leaves scars that may never heat. Species that once thrived are disappearing.

Yet, I still have hope I see compassion and understanding growing among you. I hear voices defending me and hands planting new life Stand with me as stewards of this planet Embrace sustainability, protect biodiversity, and support conservation. Educate yourselves and others about the interconnectedness of all life.
